## The Purpose and Scope

### Clarify the Problem:
- The problem we aim to solve is the difficulty people face when searching for information on the internet. Users frequently struggle to find what they want, leading to the need for detailed prompts to AI systems. Furthermore, many existing free AI solutions are suboptimal in terms of performance and speed.

### Target Audience:
- Our primary audience includes internet users frustrated with the challenges of finding information on the web. This encompasses individuals, professionals, students, and researchers who seek a more efficient and user-friendly way to locate information and answers online.

### Use Cases:
- Use Case 1: Simplifying Web Searches: Users will experience more effective and intuitive web searches, reducing the need for complex queries and increasing the relevance of search results.
- Use Case 2: Enhancing AI Prompt Responses: Our search engine will facilitate the generation of more accurate and context-aware responses to user prompts for AI systems, making interactions with AI more productive and seamless.
- Use Case 3: Streamlining Online Research: Researchers and professionals will find it easier to access and organize information for their work, saving time and improving the quality of their research.

### Unique Selling Proposition (USP):
- What sets our search engine apart is its dedication to resolving the common issues users face when searching the internet. We prioritize:
- Exceptional Search Quality: Our AI-powered search engine will deliver highly accurate and relevant search results, minimizing the need for users to resort to complex queries.
- User-Friendly Experience: We offer an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, making information retrieval straightforward for users of all backgrounds.
- Affordability: We are committed to providing a high-quality search experience without pushing users toward expensive premium versions. We prioritize accessibility and affordability for all.
